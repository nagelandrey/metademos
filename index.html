About project

Meta Demos creates a metaverse of engaged players contributing to the platform's further development by focusing on user-generated content. By introducing the MEDOS token, Meta Demos promotes decentralized governance and allows users to share their opinions and ideas about the project's development. Thanks to the technology's development, decentralized control is already becoming a must-have quality for blockchain projects. The company launched with great success and has attracted support and investments from many big names in the gaming industry.
